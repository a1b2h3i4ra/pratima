Biography

Pratima Kumari Das, born September 13, 1975 in Kumhrar, Bihar, is a prominent Indian politician and advocate. With academic credentials including a B.Sc from B.R.A.U. Gaya and L.L.B from Magadh University, she combines legal expertise with political leadership to effectively serve her constituency.

Political Vision

Championing inclusive governance, she advocates for progressive policies focused on education reform, healthcare accessibility, and women's empowerment. Her vision emphasizes community-driven development and equal opportunities for all Bihar residents.
